## About

> PolarBot is WIP as of February 26, 2026.

This is a modern Discord bot made for the PolarNL community. We are actively working on improving this bot!

This bot is made to moderate our <a href="https://discord.gg/TdYzXDzqSB">PolarNL Discord Server</a> and feature new stuff for our Discord Community. This bot will also be replacing other bots to fully optimize integration, performance and compatability with our platform.
