## About

> Please note, we are actively working on rewriting PolarLearn.

Andrei founded <a href="https://polarlearn.nl/">PolarLearn</a>, as an alternative to StudyGo and in the past years it has only been getting better and better to the point where many consider PolarLearn as a better version of StudyGo. In the early days of this project there was little attention to design work and most of the work was put into getting the core functions working.

As of now we are working on rewriting PolarLearn to keep design as one of our main new focuses while remaking some older core functions of the platform.

### Me

I have contributed little to PolarLearns actual code but I have been apart of it since the beginning. I've mostly been contributing on the Discord side but also helped serveral times and tested many new features. I've also done design work for them which has been considered but we are still working on a new design for the rewrite.

#### Future

I believe that PolarLearn will continue to grow and become it's own platform yet still providing hundreds of students with a free and open source, study software.
