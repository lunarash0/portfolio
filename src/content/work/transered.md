## About

> Transered shutdown on the 22 of February, 2026.

I founded Transered in the late December of 2025,
I needed a bot for my friend Discord server and I didn't want a bot that was behind some sort of paywall or missed really important features. Transered was 100% free and served 100+ members before it was shutdown. It was a great bot but definitely lacked in web design and did not end up looking too good after all.

Transered also acted as a multi-purpose bot removing the need of having multiple bots in your Discord server and for that reason also had a lot of features.


### Shutdown

Why was a decent bot such as Transered that was so new, shutdown?
Transered was more of a learning phase, I learned a lot from making that bot and I loved directly being able to interact with other people near instant. However, maintaining a multi-purpose Discord bot for so many people eventually becomes a hasle and I wanted to focus more on bots for specific communities that could be more tailored for their needs. There for it would also be more enjoyable for them.

#### Future
I have learned so much from Transered that I don't take it as a lose and more as a success. It worked for the time being and it was enjoyable for many. I have so many plans for other bots and Transered has really inspired me so much.
